Design Overview:
Colors:
Cream (#FDF8F8) for the background
Deep Charcoal (#1A1A1A) for text and primary elements
 and Muted Stone (#DDD9D8) for subtle container backgrounds.
Typography:
Headings: Noto Serif, a sophisticated serif Size: 4rem (64px) for Hero titles, 2.5rem (40px) for section headers. Weight: 500 (Medium).
Body: sans-serif. Size: 1.125rem (18px). Weight: 300 (Light).
Spacing: padding (8rem / 128px) between major sections and large margins.

Dark Mode Strategy:
Palette: I used Rich Obsidian (#121212) for the main background and Muted Charcoal (#1A1A1A) for containers. This creates a deep, multi-layered look that feels like an exclusive, dimly lit restaurant.
Typography: The text is rendered in Soft Bone (#F5F5F5). This provides high legibility while maintaining a softer, more luxurious feel than pure white.
Glassmorphism: I've leaned into the glass effects for the CTA buttons and cards, which work beautifully in dark mode to create a sense of depth and premium quality.
